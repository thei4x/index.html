About Capybaras!

Capybaras are gentle, social, and surprisingly charming creatures, known as the friendly giants of the rodent world who love swimming and bonding in groups.

They are:

  • Large
  • Aquatic
  • Social
  • Herbivorous
  • Gentle
  • Webbed
  • Vocal
Weight
35–66 kg (77–146 lbs)
Length
106–134 cm (3.5–4.4 ft)
Lifespan
8–12 years
Group Size
10–20 on average
